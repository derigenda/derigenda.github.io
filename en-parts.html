API to get parts of English words:
E.g. https://derigenda.co.uk/en-parts.html?word=child&part=10 returns "child's".
The "part" parameter is used as follows (leave blank to list all words):
Parameter Part Examples
1 Infinite be
2 I form am
3 You/plural are
4 3rd person sing is
5 Past singular was
6 Past plural Were
7 Participle been
8 Present Participle being
9 Plural children
10 Singular possessive child's
11 Plural possessive children's
12 Comparative happier
13 Superlative happiest
